Bear Alarm™



About

Bear Alarm™ enables ordinary people to protect their property and ensure the survival of bears in their native habitats


How We Do It

Powered by our patented Alarmingly Effective™ sound wave technology, Bear Alarm™ gives any person the power to protect themselves and their property, while helping endangered bears steer clear from harmful human contact.



First, non-invasive collars with specially coded micro-bits, or mini computers, are placed on bears. These collars function as radio beacons that can be used by other devices for locating the bears and activating alarms.

We do that part for you though.

To protect yourself, you just need to purchase a reciever device, and place it in the desired area, or on your person, select the functionality--either single alarm or display mode--and the setup process is complete.


The detectors can reach up to 1000 feet, though the devices can be calibrated to achieve more precise distances less than 5 feet.


Our bears are going extinct

According to a study by the National Park Service, the amount of bears in our preserves has gone down by 13% in the last 30 years, and are on track to reach a decrease of 15% by 2030.

And thanks to our design, purchasing a device helps bears too, stopping them from eating potentially poisonous trash that humans bring into their land.
